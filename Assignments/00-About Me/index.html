ABOUT

I was born and raised in Atlanta, Georgia. I consider myself an energetic and positive person with a bubbly personality. I can usually make anyone laugh even when I'm not trying. I'm a black feminist and I believe that everybody should be treated like a human no matter what. I'm also a concert connoiseur, foodie, and otaku. HOBBIES

Concerts

I go to concerts and music festivals on a regular basis. I feel like Atlanta is a great place for concerts because the city has so many small venues where you can go to see artists at reasonable prices. The venues are more intimate than stadium concerts. The venues that I frequent inlcude: The Masquerade, Center Stage, Variety Playhouse, The Tabernacle, and Aisle 5.

Some of the music festivals I have attended are The Red Bull Music Festival, Music Midtown, and Afropunk. Afropunk is my favorite festival because there are so many alternative black people with unique styles and I feel like I can be myself there. On the other hand, I probably won't return to Music Midtown because Piedmont Park is way too big and I didn't like enough of the artists to justify the cost.

My favorite genres are R&B and Pop and rap by women. Doja Cat, BbyMutha, Yung Baby Tate, and Junglepussy are some of the artists I enjoy. The Weeknd is one of my favorite artists as well.
